Center for bail resources in the bronx

The Bronx Defenders can help.

Arrested? Wanted by the police? Contacted by ACS? Before you talk to the cops or to ACS, talk to a lawyer! Call 347-778-1266, our free 24-hour legal emergency hotline. We can call the precinct to get information. We can stay in contact with family or friends. We can answer questions about the process.

Equal Justice Initiative

We litigate on behalf of condemned prisoners, juvenile offenders, people wrongly convicted or charged with violent crimes, poor people denied effective representation, and others whose trials are marked by racial bias or prosecutorial misconduct. EJI works with communities that have been marginalized by poverty and discouraged by unequal treatment.
