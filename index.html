Selected Works.

Her She Corp 01/2016

Her She Corp, is an established fashion company in the fashion district. They design and manufacture their products to national and international resellers at a wholesale value. My goal at Her She was to redefine their brand into a product to consumer brand. I started off with a marketing and online business plan. I designed banners, scheduled photoshoots, designed look-books, and logos in order to build their online presence. I designed and developed their website. I set up their products to be sold in Amazon and Shopify. I increased visibility on Facebook, Instagram & Pinterest. After hard work and planning, they now have both frequent wholesale and retail consumers.

The LA Dog Walkers 2016

The LA Dogwalkers is an new coming dog walking business. When I first met with the team, I noticed they were concerned about the best way to reach out to consumers around the areas they plan to work. This is when I decided to use my skills in natural language processing to develop a simple, yet effective way for users to schedule a dog walk. This ongoing project will soon be as an app in the android and iOS store and will feature a strong set of SEO plans in order to reach out to consumers appropriately.

Redefined Life 2016

Redefined Life is a new coming music group. I developed a mysterious yet interactive website with a counter that will soon countdown to their album release date.
