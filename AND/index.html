Against Neighborhood Displacement is a collective of community members that center the narratives of people experiencing gentrification and those who have been affected by physical and/or cultural displacement in central/metro LA. Our collective is also open to community members who may not live in the area but support our anti-gentrification movement. Using direct action and story telling we hope to bring awareness to the ongoing process of gentrification and our resistance to class warfare.
